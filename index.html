Bellman's World

Welcome to Bellman's world! A tribute to Richard E. Bellman. A mouse called Bellman has to reach some cake.
  • When reaching some cake cell , she win 100€ (reward of 100€).
  • When reaching some death cell , she looses 100€ (reward of -100€).
  • Each step to an empty cell costs 1€ (reward of -1€).
You can modify the map.
  • Left mouse button: increase the reward in a cell (state): death → empty → cake .
  • Right mouse button: decrease the reward in a cell (state): cake → empty → death .
