Bellman's World

Welcome to Bellman's world! A tribute to Richard E. Bellman. A mouse called Bellman has to reach some bell.
  • Reaching some bell gives her 100€ (reward of 100€).
  • Reaching some spikes hearts and she looses 100€ (reward of -100€).
  • Each step to an empty cell costs 1€ (reward of -1€).
You can modify the map.
  • Left mouse button: increase the reward in a cell (state): spikes → empty → bell.
  • Right mouse button: decrease the reward in a cell (state): bell → empty → spikes.
